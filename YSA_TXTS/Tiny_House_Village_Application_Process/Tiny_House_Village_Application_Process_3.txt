1. What is the first option to see if you qualify to take a Crisis Assessment?
2. When are you able to call Towne House?
3. When are you able to call 2-1-1?
4. Where can people without a phone call 2-1-1?
1. The first option to see if you qualify to take a Crisis Assessment is to call in.
2. You are able to call Towne House from Monday to Wednesday between 1 and 4 pm.
3. You are able to call 2-1-1 from Monday to Friday between 9 and 12 pm.
4. People without a phone can call 2-1-1 from the following locations: Berkeley Drop-In Center (3234 Adeline Street, Berkeley CA 94703) and Women’s Daytime Drop-in Center (2218 Acton Street, Berkeley CA 94702, for women only).
